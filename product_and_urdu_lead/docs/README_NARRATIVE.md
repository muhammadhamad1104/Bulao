# Bulao: Pitch Narrative

Bulao is not just another gig economy app; it's an AI-native orchestrator built specifically for Pakistan's informal economy. We solve the trust, pricing, and communication barriers that plague traditional home service apps. 

By allowing users to simply speak into the app in their native, unstructured Urdu, our 6-agent pipeline handles the complexity of parsing intent, finding providers, transparently calculating fair prices, and resolving disputes seamlessly.
